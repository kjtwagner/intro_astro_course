Welcome to a workshop series focused on introductory topics in astrophysics. Participants will learn about astrophysical observations, theory, and instrumentation in three separate 30-minute modules. Laptops will be provided for on-site use and no prior experience is needed! We have tailored the modules for inquisitive minds at the high school level and beyond, including both students and adults interested in learning more about the way astrophysics research is conducted.
